Version 1.0.2 - Stable Release

What's New in Musly v1.0.2

  • Smart Recommendations & History: Personalized mixes, "For You" section on Home, and a new listening history screen that learns from your plays.
  • Desktop Lyrics Fullscreen: Apple Music–style fullscreen synced lyrics on desktop with smooth auto-scroll and BPM-based intro dots.
  • Music Folders & Library: Choose which Subsonic music folders to include and browse new "All Albums" and "All Songs" library views.
  • Bug Fixes & Performance: Better MiniPlayer visibility on mobile, clearer login errors, and faster library caching for smoother playback.
